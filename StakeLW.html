Stake linework

Staking linework allows you to locate linear plan info. Lines are single segments, so it is more common to stake linework that may contain many segments. Most of the time, linework is drawn in 2D or a single elevation to make sure you don't use this info for vertical layout. Examples include Back of Curb, Sidewalks, Pipe Locations, etc. While staking, the information will guide you along the linework.
